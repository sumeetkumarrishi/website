Important LLM Benchmarks

Category: AI → Models

Overview

Benchmarks measure different capabilities of a model—science knowledge, reasoning, mathematics, coding, and frontier intelligence. No single benchmark tells the whole story, so it helps to look across several.

GPQA

What is it?

Google-Proof Q&A—science questions across:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology

Interesting fact

  • Non-PhD humans: ~34%
  • PhD humans: ~65%

This benchmark is designed to be difficult even when people can search the web.

MMLU-Pro

  • Successor to MMLU
  • Harder questions
  • 10 answer choices instead of 4
  • Reduced ambiguity

Because it is harder and less ambiguous than the original MMLU, companies still report it as a strong signal of general reasoning ability.

MuSR

A murder-mystery benchmark for multi-step reasoning. The model receives:

  • A 1000-word story
  • Multiple suspects

Question: Who had motive, means, and opportunity?

This is much easier for readers to understand than abstract benchmarks—it tests reasoning in a way anyone can follow.
